Common Questions

Quick answers to help you start your journey with confidence.

Is the first exam really free? expand_more
Absolutely. Your initial consultation, including a clinical exam and digital 3D x-rays, is 100% free with no obligation to start treatment.
How long does treatment usually take? expand_more
Every smile is different. Average treatment times range from 12 to 24 months, but we'll provide a more accurate estimate during your free exam.
Do you accept my insurance? expand_more
We accept most major insurance plans and our benefits coordinator will handle all the paperwork for you. We also offer flexible, $0 down payment plans to fit your budget.
